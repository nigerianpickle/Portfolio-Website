Football Simulator

Inspired by the hit game “FIFA,” this Java‑based Football Simulator lets you manage a full season of league play from the comfort of your command line or GUI. You can:

  • Create and customize up to 20 teams, each with squad rosters loaded from CSV
  • Simulate individual matches with a real‑time engine that processes tactics, player rating, and form
  • Track season standings, goal differentials, and individual player statistics (goals, assists, clean sheets)

Technologies Used:

  • Java (core engine & UI): ★★★★☆
  • SQLITE3 (persistence): ★★★★★
