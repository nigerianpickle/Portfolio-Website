Path‑Finding Java!

Path‑Finding Java! is a desktop application that brings the power of graph search algorithms to life. Users can draw walls and weighted terrain on a grid, then watch as A* and Dijkstra’s algorithms explore in real time—complete with node coloring, open/closed lists, and step‑by‑step controls.

  • Interactive Grid: Click or drag to place barriers, weighted cells, start/end points.
  • Real‑Time Visualization: Watch nodes light up as the frontier expands; adjust speed on the fly.

Technologies & Tools:

  • Java SE (core logic): ★★★★☆
  • Swing (custom Canvas & controls): ★★★★☆
