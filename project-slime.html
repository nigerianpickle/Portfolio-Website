Slime Jump

Slime Jump is a fast‑paced 2D platformer prototype built in Godot during a 48‑hour hackathon. You control a bouncy slime character, timing jumps and wall‑clings to traverse trap‑filled caverns, collect gems, and race to the exit before time runs out.
